Fly By Wire

"Anternet" was a Bio-Art piece in the 2011 Automata show. The piece involved tracking the movement of ants through an ant farm and posted messages on a social network in response to their motion.

Sirens

"Sirens -- Prototype I", created by Gumbo Labs, was a installation appearing in ‘A Technological Terrarium,’ curated by Myrtle Von Damitz, at the Ogden Museum of Southern Art.

Fly By Wire>

"Fly By Wire -- Towards a Post-Furby metamorphosis" was part of a group show, VOLATILIA, curated my Myrtle von Damitz III, at Barrister's Gallery in New Orleans. "Fly By Wire" features 1999 era Furbies whose tactile sensor are connected to the internet and who interact with the gallery patrons via twitter and text messaging.

Orpheus Descending>

"Orpheus Descending" by Jimmy Stamp, Sergio Padilla, Frederick Stivers (NO/other) and Gumbo Labs, part of DesCours 2009 Installation series sponsored by the New Orleans Chapter of the American Institute of Architects.

Helped design and implement the interactive lighting as a member of Gumbo Labs.
